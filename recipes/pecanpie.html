Pecan Pie

Description

The South's best dessert made perfect with a touch of maple.

Ingredients

  • 1 and 1/4 cups of flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 cup of shortening, chilled and diced
  • 3 tablespoons of ice water
  • 2 teaspoons of butter
  • 1 and 1/2 cups of pecans
  • 3 eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup of real maple syrup
  • 1/2 cup of white sugar
  • 1/3 cup of butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt

Steps

  1. In a medium bowl, mix together flour and salt
  2. Use a pastry blender or 2 knives to cut half of the shortening into the flour mixture, until fine crumbs form
  3. Cut the remaining shortening until coarse crumbs form
  4. Add water, 1 tablespoon at a time, tossing with fork until a dough forms
  5. Shape dough into a disk and wrap in plastic wrap
  6. Chill for 30 minutes
  7. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  8. On a floured surface, use a floured rolling pin to roll dough into an 11-inch circle
  9. Fit the dough into a 9-inch quiche dish and trim edges
  10. In a heavy skillet, melt butter over low heat
  11. Add nuts and stir for about 5 minutes
  12. Let it cool for 10 minutes
  13. Mix together beaten eggs, maple syrup, melted butter, vanilla, and salt
  14. Stir in nuts
  15. Pour filling into crust
  16. Bake for 45 to 50 minutes or until filling is set
  17. Transfer pan to wire rack and cool
